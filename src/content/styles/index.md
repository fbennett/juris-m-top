description: Jurism citations are generated by CSL-M, an extended
             version of the Citation Style Language (CSL) relied upon
             by Zotero and other leading document systems. The features
             added by CSL-M permit the accurate formatting of citations
             from multiple jurisdictions, as well as multilingual
             citation styles.
             
             The CSL-M styles listed to the right can be installed
             (or reinstalled) in a running Jurism client by clicking
             on their links in a browser (Chrome or Firefox) that has
             the Jurism Connector extension installed.
             
             Note that each JM style is capable of correctly rendering legal
             references for all jurisdictions. They differ only in decorative
             formatting and backreferencing conventions.
