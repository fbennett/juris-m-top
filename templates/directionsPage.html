Transit Directions

From Nagoya Station:

  1. Take the Higashiyama Subway Line to Motoyama Station.
  2. Transfer to the Meijo Subway Line to Nagoya Daigaku Station.
  3. Exit the station and make your way to the school (shown on the map at the top of this page).

You can also explore other options on Google Maps.

From Chubu Centrair International Airport (NGO):

  1. Take the Meitetsu Airport Line to Kanayama Station.
  2. Transfer to the Meijo Subway Line to Nagoya Daigaku Station.
  3. Exit the station and make your way to the school (shown on the map at the top of this page).

You can also explore other options on Google Maps.
